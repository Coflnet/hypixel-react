# Auction House Browser

The Auction House Browser lets you explore over 600 million historical auctions with powerful search and filtering capabilities.

## Basic Navigation

### Search
- Use the search bar to find items or players
- Search supports partial names and abbreviations
- Results are ranked by popularity

### Item Pages
Every item has a dedicated page showing:
- **Price graphs** with historical trends
- **Recent auctions** that have ended
- **Active auctions** currently available
- **Related items** for comparison

## Price Graphs

### Auction House Items
- **Average Price**: Mean sale price over time
- **Min/Max**: Price range for each time period
- **Volume**: Number of sales per day
- **Mayor Data**: Special events affecting prices

### Bazaar Items
- **Buy/Sell Prices**: Instant buy and sell prices
- **Order Volume**: Number of items traded
- **Moving Average**: Trend indicators
- **Split/Single View**: Choose your preferred layout

### Time Ranges
- **1 Hour**: Real-time bazaar data
- **1 Day**: Recent price movements
- **1 Week**: Short-term trends
- **1 Month**: Medium-term analysis
- **All Time**: Complete price history

## Advanced Filtering

### Item Filters
Filter auctions by specific attributes:
- **Enchantments**: Specific enchants and levels
- **Reforges**: Item reforge types
- **Stars**: Dungeon star levels
- **Rarity**: Item tier (Common to Mythic)
- **Price Range**: Minimum and maximum prices

### Date Filters
- **End Date**: When auctions ended
- **Creation Date**: When items were created
- **Custom Ranges**: Specific time periods

### Player Filters
- **Seller**: Specific player names
- **Buyer**: Who purchased items
- **Rank**: Player ranks and titles

## Player Profiles

### Auction History
View a player's:
- **Created Auctions**: Items they've sold
- **Bid History**: Auctions they've bid on
- **Active Auctions**: Current listings
- **Flip Tracking**: Profitable flips made

### Filtering Player Data
- Filter by item type
- Date range selection
- Price thresholds
- Auction status (sold/unsold)

## Auction Details

Each auction page shows:
- **Item Information**: Name, tier, category
- **Auction Data**: Start/end times, bids
- **Seller Information**: Player details
- **Bid History**: All bids with timestamps
- **Item Properties**: Enchantments, reforges, NBT data

### Special Features
- **Compare to Similar**: See reference auctions
- **Copy Commands**: Get in-game auction links
- **Subscribe**: Get notifications for similar items
- **Previous Owners**: Track item history (Premium+)
