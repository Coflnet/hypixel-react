# Account Setup

Setting up your Coflnet account unlocks additional features and personalizes your experience.

## Google Account Login

1. Click "Login with Google" on any page
2. Select your Google account
3. Accept the privacy policy
4. You're now logged in!

### Benefits of Logging In
- Access to premium features
- Personalized flip settings
- Notification system
- Account synchronization across devices

## Minecraft Account Verification

Linking your Minecraft account provides additional benefits:

### Why Verify?
- **Referral bonuses**: Only awarded after verification
- **Faster flips**: Unverified accounts receive delayed flips
- **Trading features**: Required for the trading system
- **Profit tracking**: Track flips across all your accounts

### How to Verify

1. Install our [Minecraft mod](/mod)
2. Join Hypixel SkyBlock
3. The mod will give you a 3-digit verification number
4. Create or bid on an auction with that number in the price
5. Your account will be automatically verified within 3 minutes

### Verification Examples

If your verification number is **123**:
- Bid **123** coins on any auction
- Bid **8,439,123** coins (number at the end)
- Create an auction for **123** coins
- Create an auction for **53,123** coins

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues
- Ensure your browser allows popups
- Try disabling ad blockers temporarily
- Use the "Reset Google login" button if needed

### Verification Problems
- Make sure you're using the exact number from the mod
- Wait up to 5 minutes for verification
- Try creating a new auction if bidding doesn't work
- Contact support if issues persist

## Security

- Your Google account is used only for authentication
- We don't store your Google password
- Minecraft verification is done through auction patterns
- All data is encrypted and secure
