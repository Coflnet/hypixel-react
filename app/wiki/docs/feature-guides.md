# SkyCofl Feature Guides

This page is the index for SkyCofl website and mod features. Every live tool linked below contains an explanation of what it does, how to read its results, risks, frequently asked questions, and related workflows.

## Flipping tools

- [Bazaar Flips](/bazaar) — live spread, price, fee, manipulation, and volume analysis. See the [complete Bazaar flipping guide](/wiki/bazaar-flips). In the mod, `/cofl bazaar` finds current flips; `/cofl bazaar history` shows tracked profit from completed flips.
- [Premium Bazaar Flips](/premiumBazaar) — demand and order-book-aware Bazaar opportunities for active traders.
- [Auction House Item Flipper](/flipper) — undervalued Auction House items, modifiers, filters, and resale analysis.
- [Craft Flips](/crafts) — ingredient trees, acquisition cost, output value, and profitable recipe chains.
- [Book Flips](/bookFlips) — enchantment-book combining costs, output tiers, demand, and profit per hour.
- [Forge Flips](/forge) — time-adjusted forge recipes, requirements, ingredients, and output value. See the [complete Forge Flips guide](/wiki/forge-flips) for the website and `/cofl forge` workflow.
- [NPC Flips](/npc) — fixed-price vendor purchases resold to the Bazaar or Auction House.
- [Reverse NPC Flips](/reverseNpc) — market purchases that can be sold to NPCs above their entry cost.
- [Kat Flips](/kat) — pet purchase, Kat upgrade cost, wait time, and upgraded-pet resale value.
- [Fusion Flips](/fusion) — attribute-shard fusion inputs, output probabilities, costs, and sale value.
- [Attribute Flips](/attributeFlips) — profitable enchantment, attribute, reforge, and item-upgrade paths.

## Market research and tracking

- [Low Supply Items](/lowSupply) — thin Auction House supply and demand opportunities.
- [Recent Flips](/recentFlips) — recently completed flips used to validate real sale behavior.
- [Top Movers](/topMovers) — recent Bazaar and Auction House price movement.
- [Profit Leaderboard](/profitLeaderboard) — long-run profit comparisons and player performance.
- [Auction House Browser](/wiki/auction-house) — current and historical auctions, item search, price history, and Bazaar history.
- [SkyBlock Flip Tracker](/guides/skyblock-flip-tracker) — public AH history, tracked profit, trades, lowballs, and Bazaar review.
